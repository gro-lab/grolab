Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Data Collection

AI Browser Assistant does not collect, store, or transmit any user data to GroLab or any third-party servers. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored exclusively in Chrome's local storage on your device. They are never sent to our servers and are only used to authenticate directly with your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, or your local Ollama instance).

Page Content

When you interact with the assistant, page content (text, structure, URLs) is sent directly from your browser to your configured AI provider's API. This data passes through the AI provider's servers according to their own privacy policies:

• OpenAI Privacy Policy
• Anthropic Privacy Policy

If you use a local LLM (Ollama), all data stays entirely on your machine.

Permissions

The extension requests these Chrome permissions:

• activeTab / tabs: Access the current tab's URL and title for context
• scripting: Inject content scripts for page interaction
• storage: Save your settings locally
• sidePanel: Display the assistant interface
• host_permissions: Interact with page content on any site you visit

Open Source

This extension is fully open source. You can review all code at github.com/grolab/ai-browser-extension to verify these privacy claims.

Contact

For privacy concerns, open an issue on GitHub.
